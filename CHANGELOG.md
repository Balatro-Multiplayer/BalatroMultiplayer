# Changelog

## Ranked Update 0.4.0

- **To Do List** — Reworked. Now pays $5 (up from $4). Target poker hand is chosen from all hands, not just discovered ones.
- **Ouija** and **Ectoplasm** now have a cost of $4 (bug fix).
- **Golden Ticket** — Reverted rarity shift, now Common rarity.
- **Baron** — Now Uncommon, $5 (down from Rare, $8).
- **Mime** — Now Rare, $8 (up from Uncommon, $5).

### Economy & Enhancements

- **Gold Card** (Enhancement) — Payout increased from $3 to $4.
- **Gold Seal** — Payout increased from $3 to $4.
- **Glass Card** — Multiplier increased from x1.5 to x2. Glass cards can now only be created through Spectral cards (Familiar, Grim, Incantation) and no longer appear in standard packs.

### Balanced Sticker

The Balanced sticker now shows a tooltip explaining what was changed.

## Ranked Update 0.3.0

### Jokers

- **Seltzer** — Lasts 8 hands instead of 10.
- **Turtle Bean** — +4 hand size instead of +5.
- **Golden Ticket** — Earns $3 instead of $4. No longer requires Gold cards in deck. Now Uncommon rarity.

### Consumables
- **Ouija** — Destroys 3 cards instead of converting all cards and losing hand size.
